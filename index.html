Malaria Data Exploration

Kenya counties in 2006 - 2013

Each line represents a county. They are colored by percentage of people that had fever or malaria in 2006.
Click and drag along an axis to filter data. Drag the axes titles around to reorder the dimensions. Explore!

For example, you can drag the Poverty Rate axis next to the Fever or Malaria column and then filter on the Fever or Malaria axis to check for correlations. While you filter and move the filter along the axis, you can also observe the selected counties on the map.

Data sources

The data sources come from the Humanitarian Data Exchange.

  • % That Had a Fever or Malaria in 2006
  • Malaria Cases per 100k people, in May 2013
  • Poverty Rate in 2006
  • Physicians Density per 100k people, in 2013
  • % That Slept Under a Bed Net in 2006
  • Health Spending Per Person in USD, in 2006
  • Number of Facilities in 2007
  • HIV prevalence for adults (15+) in 2014
  • Population in 2014
